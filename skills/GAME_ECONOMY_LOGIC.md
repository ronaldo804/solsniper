# Game Economy & Logic Skill (v1.0)
## Objective: Build balanced, addictive, and sustainable game mechanics.

### 1. Progression Calculus
- **ROI Balancing:** Calculate crop growth time vs. cost to ensure players are always challenged but rewarded.
- **Scalable Difficulty:** Increas cost/time requirements as the player levels up to prevent inflation.
- **Coin Sinks:** Implement mechanisms (like shop upgrades or cosmetic items) to remove excess currency from the ecosystem.

### 2. State Persistence
- **Anti-Cheat Logic:** Ensure critical balances are checked against server-side logic (or robust local validation).
- **Offline Progress:** Calculate growth or earnings that happened while the player was away.
- **Inventory Management:** Handle stacking, inventory limits, and item rarity logically.

### 3. Engagement Loops
- **Daily Rewards:** Incentivize daily logins with increasing rewards.
- **Urgency Mechanics:** Use limited-time offers or fast-growing rare crops to keep players active.
- **Visual Rewards:** Flashy animations and celebrations for "Level up" or "Big harvest".
