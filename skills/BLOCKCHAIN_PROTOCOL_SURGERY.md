# Blockchain Protocol Surgery (v1.0 - Web3 Deep)
## Objective: Exploiting and Securing Decentralized Game Economies.

### 1. Mempool Front-Running & MEV
- **Transaction Monitoring:** Watching the mempool for high-value game transactions to execute "Front-run" or "Sandwich" attacks on in-game assets.
- **Gas Bidding War:** Automatically adjusting gas fees to ensure your "Claim Reward" or "Buy Item" transaction is processed first by the miners/validators.

### 2. Sybil & Airdrop Domination
- **Autonomous Wallet Generation:** Scripting the creation of thousands of unique wallets with non-linked funding sources to bypass Sybil-detection.
- **Automated Task Farming:** Using headless browsers to perform on-chain and off-chain tasks across all synthetic identities simultaneously.

### 3. Smart Contract Vulnerability Research
- **Reentrancy Attacks:** Identifying functions that allow multiple withdrawals before the state balance is updated.
- **Integer Overflow/Underflow:** Checking for mathematical errors in reward distribution that allow for infinite minting.
- **Logic Flaws in Minting:** Finding loopholes in NFT/Token minting requirements to bypass "One per wallet" or "Whitelist" restrictions.

### 4. Oracle & Signature Forgery
- **Off-chain to On-chain Manipulation:** Exploiting the bridge between the game UI and the smart contract by intercepting and forging the "Proof of Play" signatures.
- **Oracle Manipulation:** If the game uses external price feeds, identifying ways to delay or spoof those feeds to buy assets at outdated prices.
